About Paws & Claws Grooming

Established in 2015, we have grown to a team of four groomers, providing professional dog grooming services. We cater to all breeds and are experienced in hand-stripping.

We are qualified to Diploma Level and City & Guilds in Dog Grooming. Additionally, we hold a Canine First Aid Certificate.

Paws & Claws is an award-winning dog grooming salon in Nottingham.
We offer a full range of grooming services, from bathing and nail trims to breed-specific haircuts and hand-stripping. Our salon is designed and equipped to the highest standard, ensuring a calm, stress-free, clean, and safe experience for every dog. We prioritize a relaxed atmosphere, allowing dogs to roam freely (no crates).
